## 🎯 KEY TAKEAWAYS

### The Core Pitch
> **Transform AutoBuddy from single-vehicle (Auto) booking to multi-vehicle platform (Auto, Taxi, XL, Mini Truck, Full Truck) in 3 weeks with 10 engineers and ₹50L budget. Expected impact: 150% revenue increase (₹200+ Cr Year 1).**

### The Technical Lift
- Build: 6 database tables, 14 API endpoints, 5 React components
- Smart dispatch matching drivers to vehicle types
- Dynamic pricing with surge management
- No breaking changes to existing Auto booking

### The Timeline
- Week 1 (June 3-7): Foundation (DB + APIs)
- Week 2 (June 10-14): Core features
- Week 3 (June 17-20): Polish & launch
- **Launch**: June 20, 2026

### The Team
- 2 Backend engineers (database + APIs)
- 2 Frontend engineers (components + UI)
- 1 QA engineer (testing + verification)
- 1 DevOps engineer (infrastructure)
- 1 Product manager (requirements)
- 2 Management (oversight + coordination)

### The Success Criteria
✅ All 6 tables created  
✅ All 14 endpoints <500ms  
✅ 5 components displaying correctly  
✅ 1000+ test bookings  
✅ 100+ concurrent users handled  
✅ 0 critical bugs  
✅ Ready for production
