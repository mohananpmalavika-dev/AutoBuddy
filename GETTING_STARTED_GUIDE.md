Welcome to AutoBuddy! This guide will help you get started quickly and smoothly.

## 🚀 First Time Setup (5 Minutes)

### Step 1: Create Account
1. Open AutoBuddy
2. Tap **"Sign Up"**
3. Enter your phone number
4. Enter the OTP (one-time code) sent to your phone
5. Enter your full name
6. Create a strong password (8+ characters)
7. Verify your email address

### Step 2: Complete Your Profile
- Add a profile picture (optional but recommended)
- Set your home address
- Add your work address (helpful for scheduling)
- Add an emergency contact
- Grant location permission (required for rides)

### Step 3: Add Payment Method
AutoBuddy accepts multiple payment methods:
- **Wallet**: Prepaid credits (best for frequent users)
- **UPI**: Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm
- **Credit/Debit Card**: Visa, Mastercard, Amex
- **Cash**: Pay driver directly (on selected rides)

**Tip**: Start with a small wallet top-up to try your first ride!

### Step 4: Choose Your Plan
Select a plan that fits your needs:
- **Basic**: Occasional riders
- **Premium**: Regular commuters
- **Pro**: Daily users

You can change your plan anytime.

## 🎯 Your First Ride (Passengers)

### Step 1: Open App & Allow Permissions
- Tap AutoBuddy
- Allow location access when prompted
- This helps us find nearby drivers

### Step 2: Enter Destination
- Tap the search box at the top
- Type your destination or speak it
- Select from suggestions
- Confirm your pickup location

### Step 3: Choose Ride Type
**Available Options:**
- 🏍️ **BIKE** - Affordable, 1 passenger
- 🚗 **ECONOMY** - Budget-friendly, 4 passengers  
- 👑 **PREMIUM** - Comfortable, 4 passengers
- 🚐 **XL** - Spacious, 5+ passengers

### Step 4: Review Fare & Book
- See estimated price
- See estimated pickup time
- Choose payment method
- Tap **"Book Ride"**

### Step 5: Wait for Driver
- You'll see driver details
- Watch live location tracking
- Chat with driver if needed
- Driver arrives in ~2-5 minutes

### Step 6: Enjoy Your Ride
- Driver picks you up
- Follow live GPS tracking
- Use emergency button if needed
- Provide destination tips if helpful

### Step 7: Rate Your Ride
- Rate driver 1-5 stars ⭐
- Add optional feedback
- View receipt & download if needed

## 🚗 Getting Started as a Driver

### Step 1: Complete Registration
1. Verify your phone number
2. Provide vehicle details:
   - Registration number
   - Vehicle type
   - Manufacturer & model
3. Upload required documents:
   - Driver's license
   - Vehicle registration
   - Insurance certificate
   - Pollution certificate

**Timeline**: Approval usually takes 24-48 hours

### Step 2: Set Up Banking
- Add bank account for payouts
- Set payout frequency
- Link preferred UPI account
- Create Stripe account for payments

### Step 3: Configure Preferences
- Select service areas (cities/zones)
- Choose ride types you accept
- Set working hours
- Select ride preferences

### Step 4: Start Accepting Rides
- Go Online
- See ride requests pop up
- Accept rides you want
- Pick up passenger
- Complete ride
- Get paid automatically

## ❓ Common Questions

**Q: What's the minimum age?**  
A: Passengers must be 18+. Drivers must be 21+ with valid license.

**Q: Can I change my payment method mid-ride?**  
A: No, select before booking. You can add methods anytime in Settings.

**Q: What if I forget something in the car?**  
A: Contact driver through app within 24 hours. We can help recover lost items.

**Q: How do I schedule a ride?**  
A: Select destination → Tap calendar icon → Choose date/time. You'll get a reminder 30 minutes before.

**Q: Can I ride with friends?**  
A: Yes! Choose XL ride or book multiple rides. Friends go to same destination.

**Q: What if driver doesn't show up?**  
A: Wait 10 minutes, then cancel. You get full refund. Contact support if issues.

**Q: How do refunds work?**  
A: Instant to wallet for most cases. Card refunds take 3-5 business days.
