Code-derived pitch

AutoBuddy

A full-stack ride operations platform built for local mobility with passenger, driver, and operator workflows, realtime dispatch, safety, fleet, enterprise, and analytics — verified from code.

Why it matters

Trust gap for passengers

Inconsistent fares, weak live updates, and opaque trip execution reduce confidence for short local rides.

Driver reliability problem

High commission models, manual payouts, and hidden economics push drivers toward informal and unreliable operations.

Operators lack control

Local teams need unified KYC, pricing, dispute resolution, support, and reporting in one platform.

AutoBuddy solution

Passenger, driver, operator in one product

Expo mobile/web passenger and driver apps plus a FastAPI admin/control layer with feature-rich routers.

Realtime dispatch and tracking

Socket.IO, heartbeat monitoring, live location updates, and retry-based driver assignment are core code features.

Safety, KYC, compliance

Document uploads, OTP flow, SOS support, audit trails, and admin safety compliance routes are implemented in code.

Ops, analytics & monitoring

  • Admin pricing, subscriptions, launch visitor workflows

What makes AutoBuddy strong

  • Built-in ride pooling, airport rides, corporate portal, and fleet profitability.
  • Driver trust system, dynamic pricing, and subscription model.
  • Real-time tracking, heatmaps, SOS safety, and operations center.
  • Monitoring, CI/CD, Docker deployment, and mobile + backend architecture already in repo.

Where AutoBuddy must close the gap

  • Market penetration and rider adoption need pilot launch evidence.
  • Driver supply density and enterprise sales must be proven in one Kerala corridor.
  • Operational maturity and repeatable revenue model are the next milestones.
