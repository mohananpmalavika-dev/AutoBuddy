# AutoBuddy Valuation Report

**Prepared for:** Investor discussion and KSUM/investor submission support  
**Prepared on:** May 29, 2026  
**Currency:** INR unless stated otherwise  
**Stage assumed:** MVP / pre-revenue / pre-pilot, based on current repository evidence

## 1. Executive Valuation Opinion

AutoBuddy's defensible current investor valuation range is:

| Case | Pre-money valuation | Post-money if raising Rs. 50 lakh | Investor ownership |
| --- | ---: | ---: | ---: |
| Investor-friendly | Rs. 3.5 crore | Rs. 4.0 crore | 12.50% |
| Recommended base case | Rs. 5.0 crore | Rs. 5.5 crore | 9.09% |
| Founder-friendly | Rs. 7.0 crore | Rs. 7.5 crore | 6.67% |

**Recommended investor position:** raise Rs. 50 lakh at a **Rs. 5.0 crore pre-money valuation**, or use an iSAFE / CCD / CCPS structure with a **Rs. 5.5 crore valuation cap** and standard investor protections.

This range is intentionally conservative because AutoBuddy has strong product execution but does not yet have audited revenue, verified ride volume, or signed institutional demand in the repository.

## 2. Important Disclaimer

This is an investor discussion report, not a statutory valuation certificate. For share issuance, FEMA, tax, ESOP, or Companies Act compliance, appoint a registered valuer, merchant banker, or qualified professional as required by Indian law.

PitchBook was requested, but no callable PitchBook data tool is exposed in this Codex session. Therefore, this report uses public market sources, repository evidence, and standard startup valuation methods.

## 3. Company Snapshot

AutoBuddy is a Kerala-first ride operations platform with:

- Passenger booking, fare estimates, ride tracking, wallet/payment hooks, and safety workflows.
- Driver onboarding, KYC, availability, ride acceptance, GPS updates, earnings, SOS, and expense tracking.
- Admin tooling for KYC, pricing, subscriptions, users, trips, financial controls, reports, and safety oversight.
- FastAPI backend, Expo mobile/web app, real-time Socket.IO layer, JWT/RBAC security, and production readiness documentation.

Current repository documents also state these operating targets:

- First 1,000 completed rides within the first quarter.
- Greater than 90% ride completion rate.
- Positive unit economics by month 6.
- Support for 10k daily active users at launch target.

## 4. External Market Context

The valuation is supported by three market signals:

1. **Large addressable market.** IMARC estimates India's shared mobility market at **USD 109.5 billion in 2025**, projected to reach **USD 191.2 billion by 2034**, a **6.20% CAGR**.
2. **Investor interest in Indian mobility remains active.** Rapido raised **USD 240 million at a USD 3 billion post-money valuation** in May 2026.
3. **Mature mobility platforms prove the revenue pool.** Uber reported **USD 193 billion in 2025 gross bookings** and strong profitability/cash-flow momentum, while public comps such as Uber, Grab, and Lyft show the category can support large listed companies.

These comps do not directly value AutoBuddy because AutoBuddy is pre-pilot. They support the market opportunity and help frame upside, not the current price.

## 5. Valuation Methodology

Early-stage valuation is not a pure DCF exercise. The most useful approach is a weighted blend of replacement cost, scorecard/Berkus-style startup value, venture upside, and strategic market optionality.

| Method | Weight | Implied pre-money range | Rationale |
| --- | ---: | ---: | --- |
| Cost-to-duplicate | 20% | Rs. 1.2 crore - Rs. 2.5 crore | Value of codebase, architecture, documentation, integrations, QA assets, and time saved. |
| Scorecard / Berkus | 35% | Rs. 3.5 crore - Rs. 6.0 crore | Strong MVP, large market, clear launch plan, but team/advisor names and pilot data still need proof. |
| Venture method | 30% | Rs. 5.0 crore - Rs. 9.0 crore | Upside from Kerala corridor launch and city-by-city scaling, risk-adjusted for pre-revenue status. |
| Strategic option value | 15% | Rs. 5.0 crore - Rs. 10.0 crore | Mobility category remains investable, with local driver economics and operational control as the wedge. |

**Weighted conclusion:** Rs. 3.5 crore - Rs. 7.0 crore pre-money.  
**Recommended base:** Rs. 5.0 crore pre-money.

## 6. Venture Method Assumptions

The venture method asks: if AutoBuddy succeeds, what could it be worth later, and what price today gives early investors a fair risk-adjusted return?

| Scenario by Year 5 | Daily rides | Avg fare | Platform take rate | Annual platform revenue | Exit/re-rate multiple | Implied terminal value |
| --- | ---: | ---: | ---: | ---: | ---: | ---: |
| Conservative | 4,000 | Rs. 160 | 12% | Rs. 2.8 crore | 2.0x - 3.0x | Rs. 5.6 crore - Rs. 8.4 crore |
| Base case | 12,000 | Rs. 170 | 14% | Rs. 10.4 crore | 2.0x - 3.0x | Rs. 20.8 crore - Rs. 31.2 crore |
| Upside | 30,000 | Rs. 180 | 16% | Rs. 31.5 crore | 2.0x - 3.0x | Rs. 63.0 crore - Rs. 94.5 crore |

For a pre-seed investor seeking roughly 5x to 8x risk-adjusted return, the base-case venture method supports a current post-money valuation around **Rs. 3 crore - Rs. 6 crore**. Combined with replacement cost, product readiness, and strategic market option value, this supports the recommended **Rs. 5.5 crore post-money** round.

## 7. Recommended Round Structure

### Option A: Priced Equity Round

- Raise: Rs. 50 lakh
- Pre-money: Rs. 5.0 crore
- Post-money: Rs. 5.5 crore
- Investor ownership: 9.09%
- Suggested instrument: CCPS or equity, subject to legal/tax advice

### Option B: Convertible / iSAFE-style Round

- Raise: Rs. 50 lakh
- Valuation cap: Rs. 5.5 crore
- Discount: 15% - 20% to next priced round
- Conversion trigger: next qualified equity financing
- Long stop: 18 - 24 months or next institutional round

**Recommendation:** use Option B if investor confidence is high but pricing is still contested. It avoids over-negotiating valuation before the KSUM/pilot milestones are complete.

## 9. Valuation Drivers

Factors that support the base-case valuation:

- Full-stack product already exists across passenger, driver, and admin roles.
- Product addresses an obvious local pain: safe, transparent, trackable local rides.
- Backend, authentication, real-time workflows, subscriptions, KYC, and admin controls are already represented in the repo.
- Kerala-first launch is more focused than a broad national launch.
- KSUM grant path can reduce dilution and validate the company before a larger seed round.
- Driver-friendly economics are strategically aligned with the direction of Indian mobility platforms.

## 10. Valuation Haircuts and Risks

Factors investors will use to push valuation down:

- No audited revenue or completed pilot metrics yet.
- Mobility is operationally heavy: supply onboarding, support, safety, disputes, and city-level execution matter as much as software.
- Competition from large ride-hailing apps, offline taxi/auto stands, and local aggregators.
- Regulatory uncertainty around ride-hailing, auto-rickshaw aggregation, payments, data, and safety.
- Unit economics depend on enough ride density to reduce cancellations and wait time.
- Team slide still needs named founders, roles, execution history, and advisors.

## 11. Investor Narrative

Use this wording in investor conversations:

> AutoBuddy is raising Rs. 50 lakh at a Rs. 5 crore pre-money valuation. The valuation reflects a working MVP, a complete passenger-driver-admin platform, Kerala-first launch focus, and the de-risking path through KSUM. We are not pricing this as a scaled mobility company yet. We are pricing it as a pilot-ready local mobility platform with a clear path to re-rate after 1,000 completed rides, verified driver supply, and early unit economics.
