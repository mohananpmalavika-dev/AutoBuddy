# AutoBuddy KSUM Submission Pack

## Recommended Application Route

Use the `deck.html` deck for investor conversations and KSUM screening. For KSUM, the safest first route is:

- **Idea Grant** if AutoBuddy is still at PoC/MVP validation stage and you need non-dilutive support to harden the product and run the first pilot.
- **Seed Support** if the company is already registered in Kerala as an LLP/Pvt. Ltd, has MVP/customer feedback, and is seeking commercialization support.

## KSUM Idea Grant Fit

KSUM's Idea Grant page says the scheme supports innovative startups at Ideation, Design, PoC, and early MVP stages, with assistance up to Rs. 3 lakh. It also says shortlisted applicants pitch before an Expert Committee, which evaluates Team, Product Innovation, Marketing, Financials, and Grant Utilisation Plan.

AutoBuddy maps to that lens:

- **Team:** founder-led product with visible engineering execution and launch plan.
- **Product innovation:** real-time ride dispatch, safety, KYC, local fare controls, subscriptions, and admin governance in one platform.
- **Marketing:** Kerala-first corridor launch with driver referrals, institutions, IT parks, hospitals, hotels, and transport hubs.
- **Financials:** hybrid platform fee, subscription, premium rides, and corporate/fleet billing.
- **Grant utilisation:** Rs. 3 lakh MVP-to-pilot plan shown in the deck.

## 2-Minute KSUM Video Script

**0:00-0:15 - Hook**  
Everyday local rides in Kerala still depend too much on phone calls, fare negotiation, weak tracking, and manual operations. Riders want trust. Drivers want fairer earnings. Local operators need control.

**0:15-0:35 - Product**  
AutoBuddy is a full-stack ride operations platform for passengers, drivers, and admins. A passenger can book and track a ride, a verified driver can accept and manage the trip, and admins can monitor KYC, pricing, payments, subscriptions, and safety events.

**0:35-0:55 - Innovation**  
The innovation is not just another booking app. AutoBuddy brings real-time dispatch, OTP ride start/end, SOS, emergency contacts, local fare configuration, subscription-based driver economics, and operational dashboards into one Kerala-ready platform.

**0:55-1:15 - Current status**  
The MVP foundation is already built in the repo: Expo mobile/web app, FastAPI backend, real-time Socket.IO updates, role-based authentication, payment hooks, safety workflows, and production-readiness documentation.

**1:15-1:40 - Grant plan**  
We are seeking Rs. 3 lakh from KSUM to harden the MVP, test cloud/maps/SMS/payment flows, onboard the first verified driver cohort, run a controlled Kerala corridor pilot, and produce measurable ride-completion, wait-time, and retention evidence.

**1:40-2:00 - Close**  
AutoBuddy's goal is to become Kerala's trust layer for everyday local rides: safer for riders, fairer for drivers, and easier for local operators to govern. With KSUM support, we can move from MVP to real-world validation.

## Submission Checklist

- Founder names, roles, phone, email, and location.
- Company incorporation status: Pvt. Ltd / LLP / OPC, if available.
- KSUM Unique ID status. Not mandatory at Idea Grant application filing, but mandatory before disbursal.
- DPIIT Startup India recognition status, if applying for Seed Support or later-stage programs.
- Product demo link or 2-minute video.
- Pitch deck PDF exported from `deck.html`.
- Fund utilisation plan and 6-month milestones.
- Revenue/investment proof, bank details, PAN, incorporation docs, and financial statements if requested after selection.
- Customer/driver feedback evidence if available.
