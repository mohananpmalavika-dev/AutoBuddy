# Revenue Model

## 1. Objective
Describe how AutoBuddy generates revenue and the key financial levers for growth.

## 2. Revenue Streams
- Commission on each completed ride.
- Driver onboarding and verification fees.
- Premium service fees for priority pickups or corporate rides.
- Potential future subscription products for drivers or fleets.

## 3. Pricing Approach
- Variable commission rate by ride category.
- Minimum per-ride service fee.
- Surge pricing for peak demand periods.

## 4. Unit Economics
- Average fare per ride.
- Commission percentage.
- Driver payout share.
- Gross margin after platform fees and payment costs.

## 5. Growth Assumptions
- Rider adoption rate.
- Driver onboarding velocity.
- Trip frequency per active user.
- Take rate improvements from premium services.
