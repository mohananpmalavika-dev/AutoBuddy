# Investor Pitch Deck

## 1. Problem
Local riders and drivers lack a modern, reliable ride-hailing service built for underserved cities.

## 2. Solution
AutoBuddy offers a mobile-first platform with real-time driver matching, safety features, and operational transparency.

## 3. Market Opportunity
- Large addressable market in growing tier-2 and tier-3 urban centers.
- Strong demand for localized ride services and flexible driver earnings.

## 4. Product
- Passenger app for easy booking and ride tracking.
- Driver app for acceptance, navigation, and earnings visibility.
- Admin dashboard for operations, compliance, and revenue monitoring.

## 5. Business Model
- Commission-based revenue per ride.
- Premium service fees and future subscription offerings.
- Scalable unit economics through driver density and ride frequency.

## 6. Go-to-Market
- Launch in target cities with referral and partner-based growth.
- Build a trusted local brand with safety and driver support.

## 7. Traction & Milestones
- MVP development complete for mobile/web clients and backend.
- Roadmap for pilot launch, market expansion, and operational scaling.

## 8. Team
- Founders with product, mobility, and technical execution experience.
- Operations and growth leadership focused on local market adoption.

## 9. Financial Ask
- Funding required to launch the pilot, expand driver onboarding, and scale platform operations.
- Use of funds: product development, marketing, operations, and safety compliance.
