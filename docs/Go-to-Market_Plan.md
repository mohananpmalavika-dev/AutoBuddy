# Go-to-Market Plan

## 1. Strategy
Outline how AutoBuddy will launch, attract users, and scale operations.

## 2. Target Markets
- Identify initial launch cities or regions.
- Focus on areas with unmet ride demand and poor existing coverage.

## 3. Customer Acquisition
- Local digital marketing and social media campaigns.
- Partner with driver communities and fleet operators.
- Offer referral incentives for riders and drivers.

## 4. Launch Phases
1. Pilot launch with limited user base.
2. Regional expansion after validating operations.
3. Growth stage with targeted promotions and service enhancements.

## 5. Sales and Partnerships
- Collaborate with local enterprises for employee travel.
- Offer partnerships with malls, hotels, and transport hubs.
- Build driver referral programs and onboarding support.

## 6. Success Metrics
- Cost per acquisition (CPA).
- Driver activation rate.
- First-month retention for passengers and drivers.
- Market share growth in launch cities.
