# Business Requirement Document

## 1. Objective
Capture the business justification, market opportunity, and strategic requirements for AutoBuddy.

## 2. Business Goals
- Acquire riders and drivers in priority service regions.
- Generate sustainable revenue through ride commissions and service fees.
- Establish operational control with admin and compliance tooling.
- Build a defensible platform around trust and local service quality.

## 3. Market Opportunity
- Growing demand for app-based ride services in tier-2/tier-3 cities.
- Underserved segments requiring safe, reliable driver communication.
- Opportunity to capture value from drivers needing transparent earnings and support.

## 4. Stakeholders
- Founders and investors.
- Operations managers.
- Drivers and riders.
- Marketing and business development teams.

## 5. Business Requirements
1. Launch a minimum viable product with core rider, driver, and admin flows.
2. Support two-sided user onboarding with role-specific verification.
3. Provide visibility into revenue, service performance, and compliance.
4. Enable local payment and wallet support with secure transaction records.
5. Scale to new service areas using the same platform architecture.

## 6. Success Criteria
- Achieve first 1,000 completed rides within the first quarter.
- Maintain >90% ride completion rate.
- Keep customer support response times under 12 hours.
- Reach positive unit economics by month 6.

## 7. Risk and Mitigation
- Driver supply shortage → onboard incentives and referral programs.
- Regulatory compliance → admin review and KYC workflows.
- Payment failure risks → support multiple payment providers and retry logic.
