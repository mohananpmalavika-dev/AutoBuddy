# Passenger Manual

## 1. Overview
How to use AutoBuddy as a passenger to request rides, pay, and track your trip.

## 2. Register and Sign In
- Create an account with email or phone.
- Verify identity if required.
- Complete your profile with pickup preferences.

## 3. Booking a Ride
- Enter pickup and dropoff locations.
- Choose ride type and review the estimated fare.
- Confirm booking and wait for driver assignment.

## 4. During the Ride
- Track the driver’s arrival in real time.
- Use chat for pickup coordination or special instructions.
- See ride status updates from pending to completed.

## 5. Payment and Receipts
- Choose your payment method before booking.
- Receive a ride receipt after completion.
- View trip history and payment details in your account.

## 6. Safety Tips
- Confirm driver and vehicle details before boarding.
- Share ride details with a trusted contact if needed.
- Report any concerns through the app immediately.
